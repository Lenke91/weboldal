What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a popular online money-making method where you earn commissions by promoting other people’s products or services. It’s a simple, yet effective way to generate passive income online.

How Does Affiliate Marketing Work?

  • First, sign up for an affiliate program.
  • You receive a unique affiliate link.
  • Share your link through various channels (website, social media, newsletters, etc.).
  • Earn commissions on every successful purchase through your link.
